## Example flow

```text
User: Run a fresh job briefing, show me the first 10 matches
Assistant: Found 10 matches from jobs fetched. Here are your top results...
User: Analyze Intermediate Full Stack Engineer at Runn, write a cover letter, and save it to my tracker
Assistant: Gap analysis complete. Cover letter drafted. Tracker save complete.
```

## Why this example matters

This single flow demonstrates the layered model clearly:

- ClawOS handles the platform frame and state continuity.
- CareerClaw handles the workflow-specific result set.
