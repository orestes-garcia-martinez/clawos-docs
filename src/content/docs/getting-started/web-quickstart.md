## Web flow

1. Sign in to `app.clawoshq.com`.
2. Open **CareerClaw** from the current skill workspace.
3. Add or update your preferences.
4. Paste or upload your resume so the platform can store extracted text only.
5. Ask for a fresh briefing.

## Best first prompts

- `Run a fresh job briefing`
- `Show me the first 10 matches`
- `Analyze this role`
- `Save this job to my tracker`

## Why the web app matters

The web app is the clearest place to understand the ClawOS model because it shows both layers:

- the **platform frame**
- the **CareerClaw mode**

That separation is intentional and should stay consistent as new skills are added.
