> **Platform first. Skill focused.** ClawOS owns the frame. The active skill owns the mode.

## What is ClawOS?

ClawOS is a multi-skill AI platform for focused professional workflows. The platform owns
identity, billing, channels, and trust boundaries. Skills such as CareerClaw provide the
workflow-specific experience inside that platform frame.
